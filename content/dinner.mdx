# Dinner Meals

<Accordion title="Asian Food">
- Mapo Tofu
  - Can add eggplant to make more filling
- Vegetable stir-fry
  - Seitan, tofu
- Soondubu
- Dak-bokkeum-tang
  - Chicken drumsticks, white onion, potato, beansprouts, green onions
  - Boil chicken, drain water, fill pot again and add all ingredients, boil for 30-35 minutes
  - Sauce: gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, jarlic, sugar, salt
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Pasta Variations">
- Pasta types:
  - Whole grain, chickpea/lentil, edamame
- Vegetable options:
  - Spinach, mushrooms, red onion
- Sauces:
  - Rao's marinara
  - Roasted veggie garlic (blend)
  - Olive oil garlic
  - Healthy alfredo
    - Blend milk, cottage cheese, parmesan, and italian seasoning
  - Note: can blend cottage cheese into any sauce for more Protein
- Protein sources:
  - Tempeh 
  - Seitan
  - Cannellini beans
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Curry">
- Brown rice
- Spices: garam masala, curry powder, cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, ground chili
- Tomato and onion/garlic as base (stir fry until fragrant)
- Vegetable options:
  - Green beans, cauliflower
- Protein sources:
  - Tofu
  - Chickpea
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mexican Food">
- Bean Quesadillas
  - High fiber tortilla
  - No salt added pinto beans
    - Stir fry some oil/garlic, add in beans and a little water, bring to a boil, then mash
    - Seasonings: cumin, lime, chili powder
  - Cheese, salsa, gaucamole, chobani plain yogurt
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="American Food">
- Vegetarian Chili
  - Vegetable base: onion, red bell pepper, carrots, jalapeno
- Black Bean Burger/Bowl
  - Romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, red onions (soak in water), pickles, banana peppers
  - King Sooper Burger Sauce
- Falafel
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Occasional Meats">
- Lemon Salmon
  - Ingredients: salmon, lemon, green beans/broccoli/brussel sprouts
  - Roast potatoes: oven at 425 for 30 minutes, coat in oil and seasonings
- Beef Chili
  - Ingredients: ground beef, onion, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, black beans, beef broth, cumin, chili powder, chugwater chili seasoning
  - Other: zucchini, eggplant
  - Toppings: cheese, greek yogurt
</Accordion>
